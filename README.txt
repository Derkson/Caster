Caster:

Caster is a pet project of mine to develop my skills as a programmer and to put a feeler out in the realm of software development. This repository may never be a finished project but with likely end up being a collection of many different ideas towards a common goal, albeit an ambigous one.

Caster is ideally meant to be a human interface with the digital world, a test at creating an online persona; the directing of any effort of mine to resolve the world of computers of it's flaws in the realms of relatability or familiarity, especially with digital construction. I don't entirely know what will become of this, a hope could be something similar to Marvel's Jarvis but the reality may be a program much less personified; but that's to point of this endevour, to test and see.
